Accessibility Statement

BulletByte Technologies is committed to making our website and services accessible and usable for everyone.

Our Commitment

We want everyone who visits the BulletByte Technologies website to be able to access information and use our services. This includes people using screen readers, keyboard navigation, screen magnifiers, voice recognition software, and other assistive technologies.

Our goal is to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA wherever it is reasonably possible. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we regularly review our content and features to identify and fix issues that may impact our visitors.

Measures We Take

Here are some of the ways we support accessibility on our site:

  • Using clear headings and structure to organize content.
  • Providing text alternatives (alt text) for informative images and icons.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation for menus, links, and buttons where possible.
  • Choosing high-contrast color combinations to improve readability.
  • Using descriptive link text instead of generic “click here.”
  • Labeling forms clearly and providing helpful instructions and error messages.
  • Using responsive design so content works on different devices and screen sizes.

Current Accessibility Status

We understand that no website is perfectly accessible to everyone. You may encounter some limitations in certain areas. When we become aware of an issue, we prioritize improvements that affect:

  • Online booking and appointment pages.
  • Service descriptions, pricing, and package information.
  • Contact forms and key policy pages such as privacy and terms.

Some parts of our site rely on third-party providers (for example: maps, booking, and payment systems). While we try to choose tools that support accessibility, we do not control all aspects of these services.

Technical Information

Our website depends on the following technologies to work with your browser and assistive tools:

  • HTML and WAI-ARIA attributes
  • CSS (Cascading Style Sheets)
  • JavaScript

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of a modern web browser.

Feedback & Support

Your feedback helps us improve. If you experience any difficulty using our website, encounter an accessibility barrier, or need information in another format, please contact us:

  • Phone: (626) 600-9012
  • Email: support@bulletbyte.co
  • Online: Use the contact form on our Contact page.

When you contact us about an accessibility issue, please include:

  • The page URL where you experienced the problem.
  • A brief description of what happened.
  • The browser, device, and any assistive technology you are using (for example, screen reader name).

We aim to respond to accessibility-related requests within 2–3 business days.

Formal Complaints

We take accessibility seriously. If you believe your concern has not been adequately addressed by us, you may have the option to raise the issue with relevant consumer protection or disability rights organizations in your area.
