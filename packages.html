Compare IT Support Plans

Simplify your IT management with flexible monthly packages. All plans include priority scheduling and discounts on additional services.

Basic Support

1 On-Site Visit

$89/month

Ideal for home users or individuals who need occasional help.

  • A cost-effective entry plan for essential maintenance and troubleshooting.
  • Includes one scheduled on-site visit per month, plus remote assistance for small fixes or quick guidance.

Standard Support

2 On-Site Visits

$149/month

Ideal for small offices with multiple devices.

  • A balanced plan combining on-site and remote support for up to two visits per month.
  • Covers security, backups, and network setup to keep your systems stable and secure.

Premium Support

3 On-Site Visits

$279/month

Ideal for growing businesses or clients managing multiple devices.

  • A proactive plan offering faster response, monthly maintenance, and priority scheduling.
  • Includes IT documentation and ongoing preventive health checks for optimal performance.

Business Elite

5 On-Site Visits

$449/month

Ideal for multi-site offices or clients requiring maximum uptime.

  • Our highest-tier plan delivers priority scheduling, proactive monitoring, and up to five monthly on-site visits.
  • Designed for clients who value consistent reliability and professional documentation of all IT assets.
